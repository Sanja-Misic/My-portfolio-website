About me

My name is Sanja Mišić, I'm 24 years old, and I live in Niš, Serbia. I’m a junior front-end developer and I am excited to continue learning and growing in this field. Ever since I was a child, I have been looking for ways to express my creativity and create. The job of a front-end developer has allowed me to do so in the best way possible. Not only does it involve the creative aspect that I enjoy, but it also has a logical side that I am deeply interested in.

I am a graduate of the Faculty of Philosophy, specializing in philosophy. My thesis explored 'Artificial Intelligence and its potential limitations - A Philosophical-Logical Perspective'. Thanks to my study of philosophy, I acquired critical and analytical thinking skills that help me a lot in problem-solving, debugging, but also in creating well-structured code.

Another thing that makes me believe that this job is perfect for me and my personality type is that it is not static but provides the opportunity for continuous learning and progress.

I enjoy solving tasks and challenges; every project is a new task and a new challenge that needs to be solved. My analytical nature allows me to approach projects systematically. I break down every project into smaller tasks and solve them gradually, step by step, paying attention to details.

When I'm not in front of the computer, I express my creativity in a different way. I do nail design, where I focus on details and aesthetics. In addition to that, I enjoy dancing and doing fitness, which helps me maintain a balance between physical and mental activity. Besides, I love spending time in nature and going for walks, where I absorb energy and recharge my batteries for further work.

You can find more about my experience and education in my CV.
